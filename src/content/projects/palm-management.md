A full-stack web application built for palm oil estates and mills. The system tracks Fresh Fruit Bunches (FFB) through weighing, grading, processing, and reporting — replacing ad-hoc spreadsheets with structured data capture.

## What it does

- **FFB tracking** — weight-in, weight-out, grading per consignment, traceability back to source.
- **Inventory & production** — stock positions, daily throughput, yield calculation.
- **Reporting** — operational dashboards for estate managers and mill supervisors.

## Engineering highlights

- Designed the relational schema around how estates actually move product through the mill — not the other way around.
- Implemented authentication and role-based access control so each user sees only what they need.
- Built audit tracking from day one — every weight reading is attributable to a user and a timestamp.
