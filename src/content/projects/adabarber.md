A community-driven directory of barbershops, built as a tribute to the Malaysian coffee map project **petakopi.my**. Browse nearby shops on an interactive map, with clustering and popups that show operating hours and services.

## Engineering highlights

- **NestJS backend** — REST endpoints for shops, owners, and submissions.
- **SvelteKit frontend** — fast, lightweight browsing experience.
- **GeoJSON-first** — every shop is a geographic feature, so map interactions stay simple and performant at scale.
- **Clustering** — keeps the map readable when many shops overlap.
